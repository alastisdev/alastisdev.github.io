This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games and applications developed and published by Marek Banaś under the studio name Alastis Dev — including Shadow Blocks, Shadow Slash, Shadow Raid, and Operation Shadow.

01 INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

As a core principle, Alastis Dev aims to collect as little user data as possible. Game progress and settings are saved purely locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account to play our games.

//

EXCEPTION: Shadow Slash leaderboard requires a player nickname and score. No other personal data is collected.

02 LEADERBOARD DATA

Shadow Slash features an optional global leaderboard. If you choose to submit your score, the following data is stored on our secure server (Firebase / Google Cloud):

  • Player nickname (chosen by you)
  • Score (integer value)
  • Date of submission
//

No device identifiers, IP addresses, or personal information are stored alongside leaderboard entries.

03 THIRD-PARTY SERVICES AND ADVERTISING

Our free games may incorporate third-party advertising networks (Google AdMob). These third parties may use cookies and device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) to collect non-personal data for tailored advertisements and analytics.

Players may permanently remove all advertisements via the in-app purchase remove_ads. Once purchased, no ad SDK calls are made.

//

Ad data processing is governed by Google AdMob's privacy policy.

04 IN-APP PURCHASES

Some of our games offer optional In-App Purchases. All payment processing and financial data management are handled securely and exclusively by Google Play Services.

⚠ ALASTIS DEV NEVER HAS ACCESS TO YOUR CREDIT CARD OR BILLING INFORMATION.
05 CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our Services are not designed to knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Age ratings vary per game — please refer to the official rating on the Google Play Store for each title.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

06 DATA RETENTION AND DELETION

Local save data can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the application. Leaderboard entries can be removed upon request by contacting us with your player nickname.
