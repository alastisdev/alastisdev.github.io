Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games and applications ("Services") developed and published by Marek Banaś under the studio name Alastis Dev (including but not limited to Shadow Blocks, Shadow Raid, Code name: Shadow, Reign of Shadow).

1. Information Collection and Use

As a core principle, Alastis Dev aims to collect as little user data as possible. For most of our games, game progress and settings are saved purely locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account to play our games.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Our free games may incorporate third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob). These third parties may use cookies, device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), and similar technologies to collect non-personal data to provide tailored advertisements and perform analytics. This data processing is governed by the respective privacy policies of these providers.

3. In-App Purchases

Some of our games offer optional In-App Purchases. All payment processing and financial data management are handled securely and exclusively by Google Play Services. Alastis Dev never has access to your credit card or billing information.

4. Children's Privacy and Age Limits

The target audience and age rating vary depending on the specific game (please refer to the official age rating on the Google Play Store for each title). However, our Services are not designed to knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the Developer at: alastisdev@gmail.com
