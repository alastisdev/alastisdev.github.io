Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games and applications ("Services") developed and published by Marek Banaś under the studio name Alastis Dev (including but not limited to Shadow Blocks, Shadow Raid, Code name: Shadow, Reign of Shadow).

01 // Information Collection

As a core principle, Alastis Dev aims to collect as little user data as possible. For most of our games, progress and settings are saved purely locally on your device. We do not require account creation to access our Services.

02 // Third-Party Services

Our free games may incorporate third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob). These providers may use device identifiers (Android Advertising ID) for analytics and tailored advertisements.

This data processing is governed by the respective privacy policies of these providers.

03 // In-App Purchases

Payment processing for optional In-App Purchases is handled securely and exclusively by Google Play Services.

Alastis Dev never has access to your credit card or billing information.
