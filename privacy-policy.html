Umbrella Privacy Policy

Protocol Active: April 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games and applications ("Services") developed and published by Marek Banaś under the studio name Alastis Dev (including but not limited to Shadow Blocks, Shadow Raid, Code name: Shadow, Reign of Shadow).

1. Information Collection and Use

As a core principle, Alastis Dev aims to collect as little user data as possible. For most of our games, game progress and settings are saved purely locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account to play our games.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Our free games may incorporate third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob). These third parties may use device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) to provide tailored advertisements. Data processing is governed by the respective privacy policies of these providers.

3. In-App Purchases

Some of our games offer optional In-App Purchases (e.g., "Support Developer"). All payment processing is handled securely and exclusively by Google Play Services. Alastis Dev never has access to your financial information.
